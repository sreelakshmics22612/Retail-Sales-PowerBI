# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard — Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI.
It provides insights into sales performance, product trends, customer behavior, and future forecasts.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance
* Identify top-performing products
* Understand customer segments
* Evaluate payment trends
* Forecast future sales
* Simulate discount impact on profit

---

 Dataset

Sample retail sales dataset including:

* Orders
* Customers
* Products
* Regions
* Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount
* Payment Modes

---

 Dashboard Pages

 Page 1 — Executive Overview

* KPI cards (Sales, Profit, Orders, AOV)
* Sales trend over time
* Regional performance
* Segment distribution
* Geographic sales map

### 📦 Page 2 — Product Analysis

* Top 10 products by sales
* Category & subcategory breakdown
* Quantity analysis
* Product profitability
* Drill-down insights

Page 3 — Customer Insights

* Sales by customer segment
* Payment mode distribution
* Top customers
* Discount behavior
* Customer detail table

Page 4 — Forecast & Performance

* Sales forecasting
* Profit vs sales analysis
* Discount impact study
* What-If scenario simulation
